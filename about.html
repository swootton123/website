About Us

DVO-AI is more than just a tech company—it's a mission-driven initiative to empower businesses to work smarter, not harder. Founded by Sean Wootton, a visionary programmer and innovator, DVO-AI is here to change the way companies operate, one process at a time.

About Sean Wootton

Sean Wootton has spent over six years mastering the art and science of programming, with expertise in Python, HTML, CSS, PHP, Java, and JavaScript. From building efficient systems to crafting elegant code, Sean's passion lies in creating solutions that simplify complexity. Currently pursuing his education in community college, Sean is channeling his knowledge and creativity into building AI tools that help businesses thrive in an ever-evolving digital landscape.

But it’s not just about technology—it’s about people. Sean believes that when businesses can eliminate repetitive, mundane tasks, they unlock the true potential of their teams. He envisions a world where companies of all sizes, from startups to enterprises, can focus on innovation, creativity, and human connection while DVO-AI handles the rest.

Our Mission

At DVO-AI, our mission is simple yet powerful: to help businesses save time, optimize workflows, and achieve more with less effort. We are passionate about creating AI tools that are intuitive, reliable, and transformative. Whether it’s managing emails, streamlining calls, or automating workflows, our goal is to give companies back their most valuable resource—time.

We believe that when businesses succeed, communities thrive. That’s why we are committed to providing cutting-edge technology with a personal touch, ensuring every solution we offer is tailored to the unique needs of our clients. At DVO-AI, your success is our purpose, and together, we’re building a brighter, more efficient future for businesses everywhere.
